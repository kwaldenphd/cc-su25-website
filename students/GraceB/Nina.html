Nina

This is Nina! Technically, Nina is my grandparent's dog, but they live with us in TN and it would be remiss of me to exclude her. Nina is a total sweetheart. Whenever anyone walks into the room she'll always go over to them the moment she notices their presence to say hello and press up against their legs. Nina also has the most high pitched bark I've ever heard. It practically reverberates in your head for several seconds after she does it. Despite her ear-piercing bark, Nina is beloved to all in the house.
